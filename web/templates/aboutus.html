About GPT-Engineer

GPT-Engineer is an innovative web application that simplifies the process of generating code based on a prompt. Our AI-powered system enables you to easily adapt and extend your codebase according to your preferences and desired coding style. By specifying what you want to build, the AI asks for clarification and constructs the code for you.

We believe in making programming more accessible and efficient. GPT-Engineer allows you to get value quickly with its simple yet powerful features. You can also add your own "AI steps" to customize and enhance the code generation process further. With incremental improvements and fast handovers between AI and humans, we aim to provide an excellent user experience.
